Our Process

From Fire to Table

Every dish at Cue follows the same uncompromising path from raw ingredient to your plate.

01
🧂

The Rub

Our proprietary spice blend is hand-mixed in-house. Each protein gets its own unique combination of heat, sweet, and savory.

02
💨

The Smoke

Hickory and oak logs. Low heat. Long hours. Our pitmasters tend fires through the night so your lunch is perfect.

03
🔪

The Slice

Every brisket is hand-sliced to order. Every rack is cut with care. We don't batch — we craft.

04
🫗

The Sauce

House-made sauces in multiple styles — tangy Carolina, sweet Kansas City, spicy Texas heat. Your choice, always.

05
🍽️

The Serve

Plated with pride, paired with scratch-made sides, and served with Southern hospitality. That's the Cue Standard.

Your Night, Scene by Scene

Step into the evening before it happens. This is what hosting at The Hickory Room feels like.

01

The Arrival

Your guests step through the entrance into warm amber light. The aroma of hickory smoke and slow-cooked brisket greets them before anyone says a word. The room is set. The night is yours.

🥂
02

Welcome Cocktails

Craft cocktails and conversation flow. A welcome table with passed bites sets the tone — relaxed, social, already memorable. Guests start to feel it: this is going to be a great night.

The Feast Begins

Brisket boards land on the table. Sides arrive in cast iron. The pitmaster's work takes center stage. Plates are full, the room hums with the sound of people enjoying something real.

💬
04

Someone stands up. The room quiets. Words that matter fill the space. Glasses raised. A moment that locks into memory — the kind people talk about years later.

🍰

Banana pudding shooters. Peach cobbler. Coffee and bourbon nightcaps. The music softens. Conversations deepen. The best part of the night is often the last hour.

Our Story

Built on Fire.
Made to Gather.

Cue started with a simple belief: barbecue should be made the hard way. No shortcuts. No pre-made anything. Every rub mixed by hand. Every meat smoked low and slow over real wood. Every side made from scratch, every single day.

What began as a single smokehouse has grown into three Metro Atlanta locations — Milton, Cumming, and Peachtree Corners — each with the same commitment to craft, community, and the kind of gathering that only happens around great food and live music.

We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the best your neighborhood has ever tasted.
